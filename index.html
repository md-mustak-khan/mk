About Me

I am currently pursuing my graduate studies in the Department of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology at the University of Chittagong. My academic journey has been marked by a deep passion for biological research, particularly in areas where cutting-edge science intersects with real-world applications.

Research Assistant

I am currently working as a research assistant at the Laboratory of Microbial Research, where my primary focus lies in studying microalgae and the model organism Caenorhabditis elegans (C. elegans). My responsibilities include culturing microalgae for maintenance and research purposes. This role has greatly enhanced my expertise in laboratory techniques and collaborative research.

CHIRAL Bangladesh

About a year ago, I joined CHIRAL Bangladesh, a multidisciplinary research organization renowned for its diverse scientific endeavors. Within CHIRAL, I contribute to The Laboratory of Artificial Intelligence in Health (aiHealth Lab), its research wing dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare through cutting-edge, data-driven solutions. Our mission is to harness the power of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and advanced data analytics to address pressing challenges in public health, clinical care, and health systems management.

BioPC—Bioinformatics Lab

In addition to my laboratory research, I serve as the Head of the Extracurricular Committee and a research assistant at BioPC—Bioinformatics Lab of Research and Training. This organization focuses on computational approaches to biological research, particularly in drug design, vaccine development, and cancer bioinformatics.

Through these experiences, I have developed a comprehensive research profile that bridges experimental biology and computational science. I am committed to advancing scientific knowledge through interdisciplinary research, continuous learning, and collaboration with like-minded professionals.
