Academic Contributions

This page showcases my peer-reviewed publications, conference presentations, and other scholarly contributions to the fields of biochemistry, molecular biology, and bioinformatics.

Current Publication Status

I currently do not have any publications, but I am actively working on publishing research based on my research areas. My focus is on topics related to C. elegans, Plant research, and Bioinformatics.

I aim to contribute valuable insights to the scientific community through my upcoming research publications. Please check back regularly for updates on my published work.

Research Focus Areas

C. elegans Research

Investigating the neuroprotective effects of plant-derived compounds using C. elegans as a model organism. This research aims to identify and characterize bioactive compounds that may contribute to novel pharmacological applications.

Plant Research

Analyzing the nutritional composition and antioxidant properties of various plant samples, including seaweeds and dates. This research supports the development of functional foods and nutraceuticals from locally available plant resources.

Bioinformatics Research

Utilizing computational and systems biology approaches to accelerate drug discovery, optimize vaccine design, and perform comprehensive pan-cancer analysis. Our work integrates multi-omics data to uncover novel therapeutic targets.

Future Publications

Based on my ongoing research projects, I anticipate submitting several manuscripts for publication in the coming months. These will cover findings from my work with C. elegans models, nutritional analysis of plant-derived compounds, and computational approaches to drug discovery.

My research aims to bridge experimental biology with computational methods, contributing to both fundamental scientific understanding and practical applications in medicine and nutrition.
